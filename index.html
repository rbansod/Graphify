Doc Atlas reads a web page or document and produces two views of it: a table of contents rebuilt from its heading structure, and a topic graph showing which subjects the text keeps returning to, how often, and which ones travel together.

  • Everything runs in your browser — nothing you load is sent to a server owned by this app.
  • Cross-origin pages are fetched through a public CORS proxy; a few sites block this.
  • PDF headings are inferred from font sizes, so scanned or unusual PDFs may show a flat outline.
